# Akuvox Local Integration Wiki

Welcome to the **Akuvox Local Integration** wiki for
[Home Assistant](https://www.home-assistant.io/).

This integration provides local control of
[Akuvox](https://www.akuvox.com/) intercoms and access control devices
without cloud dependencies.
